Embeddings are dense vector representations of text that place
semantically similar pieces of text close together in vector space.
Sentence embedding models such as BAAI/bge-small-en-v1.5 map a sentence to
a fixed-size vector. In retrieval, both documents and queries are embedded,
and similarity (often cosine similarity) is used to rank documents. FAISS
is a library for efficient similarity search over large collections of
vectors, and is commonly used as the vector store in RAG systems.
